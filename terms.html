What the Bot Does

Serializd Tracker Bot provides the following functionality inside Discord servers:

  • ▸ Automatically polls public Serializd.com diary entries for usernames configured by server administrators, and posts them to a designated Discord channel approximately every 5 minutes.
  • ▸ Provides interactive slash commands for browsing Serializd profiles, viewing server and global statistics, and managing bot configuration.
  • ▸ Announces upcoming and newly aired episodes for shows on your server's watching list.
  • ▸ Allows users to voluntarily link their social media profiles (Serializd, Letterboxd, Instagram, X, and others) via the /sharelink command.

The Bot only accesses publicly available data on Serializd.com. It does not interact with private or authenticated Serializd accounts.
