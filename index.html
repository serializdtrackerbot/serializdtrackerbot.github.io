Features of Serializd Tracker Bot

Everything you need to track and share TV shows in your Discord server.

📝

Automatic Diary Posting

Auto-tracks Serializd users in your server. Posts new diary entries to your designated channel within 10 minutes. Beautiful embeds with show artwork, ratings, and episode details.

🔍

Interactive Profile Browser

Browse any user's Serializd profile directly in Discord. View: Recently Logged, Currently Watching, Watchlist, Dropped, Paused. Pagination and sorting options.

📊

Global Statistics & Top 20 Leaderboards

Server stats - see what your community is watching. Global stats with Top 20 leaderboards for watchers, guilds, and trending shows across all servers. Anonymous mode available.

📦

Group Diary for Binges

Automatically groups multiple episodes into one clean embed when you binge-watch shows. Configurable time window and customizable formatting.

🔔

Episode Alerts & Upcoming

Get notified about upcoming episodes for shows you're tracking. Never miss a new episode of your favorite shows.

🎨

Fully Customizable Embeds

Custom embed colors to match your server's theme. Toggle field visibility, rename labels, configure formatting, and set thumbnail priorities.

🚀

Interactive Setup Wizard

New /setup command guides you through configuration step-by-step. Configure channels, users, and customization in 2-3 minutes.

🔗

Username Linking System

Connect your Discord account to your Serializd profile with /username. Per-server connections for privacy.

🔒

Privacy Controls

Control your data with username anonymization and Discord invite link toggles. Your privacy, your choice.

1

Add Bot to Your Server

2

Run Setup Wizard

Type /setup in your server to launch the interactive setup wizard. It will guide you through configuring the bot step-by-step.

3

Add Users to Track

Use /adduser [username] to start tracking Serializd users. The bot will automatically post their diary entries to your designated channel!

Privacy & Data Collection

We prioritize your privacy. Here's what we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Server Configuration: Guild ID, name, and configured channel IDs
  • User Tracking: Serializd usernames you choose to track
  • Activity Data: Diary entry IDs already posted
  • Technical Cache: TV show status data from TVMaze API

What We DON'T Collect

  • Message content or conversation history
  • User passwords, tokens, or credentials
  • Private Serializd account data
  • IP addresses or device information

How We Use Your Data

  • Functionality: Track and post Serializd diary entries
  • Statistics: Generate viewing stats for your community
  • Performance: Cache show data to reduce API calls
  • No Selling: Data is never sold or shared

Data Retention & Your Rights

  • Auto-Delete: All data deleted 48 hours after bot removal
  • View Data: Use commands like /listusers, /privacy
  • Full Deletion: Request via support server
